Co-PI Award for Carbon-Sequestering Concrete Research Project

2024-07-15

On July 2024, Dr. Seok, as a Co-PI, was awarded the Global Basic Research Lab Research Grant (No. RS-2024-00408370) from Korea's Ministry of Science and ICT. The title of the awarded research project is "Development of Carbon-Sequestering Concrete Using Biochar-Based Graphene and Its Application in Structural Components. Our BRL team (PI: Dr. Wonchang Choi @ Gachon Univ.; Co-PIs: Dr. Soo-Yeon Seo @ KNUT Univ., Dr. Chan Ho Park @ Gachon Univ., and Dr. Seungwook Seok @ Gachon Univ.) will receive research funds of approximately 1.05 million US dollars from August 2024 to April 2027. Congratulations!
